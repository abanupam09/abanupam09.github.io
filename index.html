Welcome to the Digital Library of Planning & Research

Planning & Research Division is currently being led by two prominent female figures being Smt. Jaya Sett, Jt. Director Planning & Research and Smt. Kaberi Chattopadhyay, Officer on Special Duty who had chaired the position of Jt. Director Planning & Research (In-charge) erstwhile. This division continuously strives to collect, compile and compose data that sets a formidable outcome of growth in respect of Eastern Maritime Corridor, Cargo, Capex, Monthly Demi-Officials, Operational Performance Reports, Projects related with Ministry of Port, Shipping and Waterways ex. Sagarmala, Sagarmanthan, Viksit Bharat Sankalp (ViBhaS), goals of the Maritime India Vision 2030 (MIV 2030), Navigation with Indian Constellation (NavIC), One Vision One Document, updation of key points on the Sagar Gyankosh Portal & Project monitoring (internally) of Departments/ Divisions of SMPK.
